Nick Whetstone | Engineer

I'm a twenty-one-year-old senior pursuing Bachelor of Science degrees in both Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering at Southern Illinois University Carbondale (SIUC), an ABET-accredited research university. In May 2014, I completed an Associate of Science Degree at Wabash Valley College before transferring to SIUC for Fall Semester 2014.

Two personal passions draw me to software engineering--building new things and learning new things. Programming presents a rare opportunity to be both technical and artistic, which, to me, explains why its popularity as both a profession and a hobby has grown so exponentially.

I am looking for full-time employment for after I complete my undergraduate studies in May 2017. If you'd like to contact me, please see the Contact Me section of this page. I'm always looking to improve, both as an engineer and as a person, and I embrace opportunities to do so.
